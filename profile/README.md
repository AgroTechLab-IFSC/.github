### AgroTechLab (Technology Development Laboratory for Agribusiness)

😁 The [AgroTechLab](https://agrotechlab.lages.ifsc.edu.br) is a multidisciplinary laboratory (computer science, agronomy and business administration) that develop technologies for agribusiness.<br>
🏫 It is part of [IFSC (Federal Instititute of Santa Catarina)](https://www.ifsc.edu.br) in southern Brazil.<br>
🌐 The laboratory is also responsible for managing the TTN community in the Lages city ([TTN/Lages](https://www.thethingsnetwork.org/community/lages/)).<br><br>
